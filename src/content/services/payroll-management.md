## The Risks of DIY Payroll

Payroll mistakes can be costly. Common errors include:

- **Late tax deposits** that trigger IRS penalties and interest
- **Misclassifying employees** as independent contractors
- **Incorrect withholding calculations** that leave employees or your business in a bind at tax time
- **Missing local tax obligations** — many Ohio municipalities have their own income tax requirements

With PJS Tax handling your payroll, you eliminate these risks and gain peace of mind knowing every detail is handled by experienced professionals.

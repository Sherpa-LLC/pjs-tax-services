## Strategic Financial Consulting in Wilmington, Ohio

Smart financial decisions start with expert guidance. At PJS Tax Accounting Service, our financial consulting services help individuals and businesses in Wilmington, Clinton County, and the surrounding area plan strategically, minimize tax exposure, and position themselves for long-term growth.

With over 25 years of professional experience, our consulting goes beyond basic number crunching. We take the time to understand your goals, analyze your financial picture, and deliver actionable recommendations you can implement with confidence.

## What's Included

- **Tax Planning & Strategy** — Proactive planning to minimize your annual tax liability through legal deductions, credits, and timing strategies.
- **Business Entity Selection** — Guidance on choosing the right structure (sole proprietorship, LLC, S-corp, partnership) for tax efficiency and liability protection.
- **Financial Forecasting** — Revenue and expense projections to help you plan for growth, manage cash flow, and make informed decisions.
- **Retirement Planning Support** — Strategies for maximizing retirement contributions and minimizing the tax impact of withdrawals.
- **Startup & New Business Advisory** — From EIN registration to chart of accounts setup, we help new businesses start on the right financial footing.

## Who Benefits from Financial Consulting

- **Small business owners** looking to reduce their tax burden and improve profitability
- **Entrepreneurs** planning a new venture and needing guidance on structure, funding, and compliance
- **Individuals and families** navigating major life changes — marriage, home purchases, inheritance, or retirement
- **Established businesses** seeking to optimize operations, evaluate expansion opportunities, or prepare for succession

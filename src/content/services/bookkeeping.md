## Professional Bookkeeping in Wilmington, Ohio

Keeping accurate financial records is the foundation of every successful business. At PJS Tax Accounting Service, our reliable bookkeeping services keep your financial records organized and compliant, giving you the clarity you need to make confident business decisions.

We understand that small business owners in Wilmington and Clinton County wear many hats. Let us take the bookkeeping burden off your plate so you can focus on what you do best — running your business.

## What's Included

- **Transaction Recording & Categorization** — We meticulously track every transaction and ensure it's properly categorized in your chart of accounts.
- **Bank & Credit Card Reconciliation** — Monthly reconciliation to catch discrepancies, prevent fraud, and keep your books aligned with your bank statements.
- **Accounts Payable & Receivable** — We track what you owe and what's owed to you, helping maintain healthy cash flow.
- **Financial Reporting** — Timely, accurate reports including profit & loss statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements so you always know where your business stands.
- **Compliance Assurance** — We ensure your records meet all federal, state, and local regulatory requirements.

## Why Accurate Bookkeeping Matters

Poor bookkeeping doesn't just create headaches — it can cost you real money. Without organized records:

- You miss tax deductions you're entitled to
- Cash flow problems sneak up without warning
- Tax season becomes a last-minute scramble
- Audits become far more stressful and expensive

With PJS Tax handling your books, you'll have clean, organized records ready for tax season, investor inquiries, or loan applications at any time.

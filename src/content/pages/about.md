## Our Story

PJS Tax Accounting Service Inc. was founded with a simple mission: to prepare accurate tax returns at the most reasonable price, ensuring our clients receive the maximum benefits they deserve. Located in Wilmington, Ohio, we have grown from a small practice into a trusted name across Clinton County and the surrounding communities.

With over 25 years of combined professional experience, our team understands the unique financial challenges faced by individuals, families, and small businesses in southwestern Ohio. We have built our reputation on precision, integrity, and genuine care for every client who walks through our door.

## Leadership

**Pamela Strong, CEO** — Pamela founded PJS Tax Accounting Service Inc. with a passion for helping people navigate the complexities of the tax system. Her hands-on approach and commitment to client satisfaction have been the driving force behind the firm's growth and its outstanding 97% client retention rate.

## Our Values

Everything we do at PJS Tax is guided by three fundamental principles:

- **Integrity** — We handle your finances with the same care we give our own. Honest advice, transparent pricing, and ethical practices are non-negotiable.
- **Professionalism** — Our team maintains current knowledge of the latest tax regulations, accounting standards, and compliance requirements. We invest in ongoing education so you receive the most up-to-date service.
- **Client Satisfaction** — Your success is our success. We take the time to understand your unique situation and tailor our services to your specific needs.
